I am a Lecturer at the Department of Informatics at the University of Leicester. Prior to that I was a Research Fellow at The University of Texas at Austin, where I worked with Prof. Ufuk Topcu. Before that I spent four years at Max Planck Institute for Software Systems working with Prof. Rupak Majumdar. I obtained my Ph.D. at Saarland University under the supervision of Prof. Bernd Finkbeiner.
