I am a Lecturer (Assistant Professor) at the Department of Computer Science at the University of Sheffield.

Prior to that I was a Lecturer at the School of Informatics at the University of Leicester. Previously, I was a Research Fellow at The University of Texas at Austin, where I worked with Prof. Ufuk Topcu. Before that I was a postdoc at Max Planck Institute for Software Systems working with Prof. Rupak Majumdar. I obtained my Ph.D. at Saarland University under the supervision of Prof. Bernd Finkbeiner.
